Dashboard Summary

The 6:01 Report dashboard serves as a comprehensive Bailment Pool Forecast Tracker designed to monitor and analyze invoice and RAF (Ready for Final) performance metrics across multiple business units.

Key Features:

  • Time-based Analysis: All charts use Invoice & RAF Date for temporal tracking
  • Multi-dimensional Filtering: Three slicers enable filtering by Site, Business Unit, and Date
  • Business Unit Breakdown: Matrix table provides cross-sectional analysis across Fire, VAN, STC, FCV, and Others
  • Comprehensive Metrics: Tracks units, hours, and invoice amounts across various RAF categories

Primary Use Cases:

  • Monitoring daily/weekly RAF unit completion rates
  • Tracking invoice amounts and billing performance
  • Comparing performance across different business units
  • Analyzing RAF hours allocation across service categories

This dashboard pulls data from the Excel.Total Fouts source and is built on the Bailment Pool Forecast Tracker model, providing real-time insights for operational and financial decision-making.
